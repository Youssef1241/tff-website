About Us

Dr. Marlene Bastawros

Founder, "Towards Functional Families"

Holder of an M.B.B.CH from the Faculty of Medicine, Cairo University, and a Master’s degree in Psychiatry and Neurology from Kasr El Aini, Cairo University, as well as a Clinical Psychiatry Diploma from Abbasia Mental Hospital. She is a certified psychiatrist holding advanced qualifications in psychiatry and clinical practice.

She started her career at El Rakawy Mental Hospital, followed by El Abassia Mental Hospital and Behman Psychiatric Hospital.

She is the founder of Towards Functional Families, which provides multiple programs for different family members, aiming to promote healthy parenting and marriage.

Since 2009 and continuing to the present, she has been running mothers’ groups, facilitating the growth and development of a team of mothers who are now leading training programs for other mothers.

She provides lectures and customized workshops for different organizations and NGOs, including UN Women, UNFPA, and the National Council for Women. She also lectures teachers and parents in various French and English schools in Cairo.
